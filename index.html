Breathing Exercises

This website provides visual aides for performing simple breathing exercises. It is not a substitute for medical advice, so please see a medical professional if you have any health concerns.

For each style you can choose the duration of each phase of the exercise. This is not a challenge, the goal is to find the exercise with which you are most comfortable.

In-out breathing

  • In for four, out for six
  • In for five, out for seven
  • In for six, out for eight

Square breathing; hold your breath between the inhale and exhale

  • In for four, hold for four, etc.
  • In for five, hold for five, etc.
  • In for six, hold for six, etc.
